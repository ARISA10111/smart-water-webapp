About Smart Water

Our story, mission, and the people behind the platform

🎯

Our Mission

To provide reliable, affordable, and timely water delivery services to homes and businesses across Kenya — removing the stress of water shortages by connecting customers with trusted local vendors through a simple digital platform.

🌍

Our Vision

To become Kenya's most trusted water delivery platform — expanding from Nairobi to every county in the country, ensuring that no home or business ever runs out of clean water.

Our Objectives

What we set out to achieve every day

🤝
Connect Customers with Vendors

Build a transparent marketplace that links customers directly with verified, local water vendors.

💧
Improve Access to Clean Water

Reduce barriers to clean water access, especially in under-served urban and peri-urban areas of Kenya.

📦
Support Efficient Distribution

Help vendors manage deliveries efficiently through the platform, reducing waste and improving response times.

Our Story

Smart Water was founded in 2024 after our founders experienced first-hand how difficult it was to find a reliable water vendor during a dry season shortage in Nairobi. Phone calls went unanswered, prices were inconsistent, and deliveries were unreliable.

We built Smart Water to solve that problem — a single platform where customers can browse verified vendors, compare prices, and place orders with confidence. Today we serve over 500 customers across Nairobi, Kiambu, and Nakuru counties.
