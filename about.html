About Smart Water

Connecting Kenya with clean water since 2024

🎯 Our Mission

To provide reliable, affordable, and timely water delivery services to homes and businesses across Kenya by leveraging technology to connect customers with trusted, vetted water vendors.

🌍 Our Vision

To become Kenya's most trusted water delivery platform — making access to clean water as easy as ordering food, and ensuring no household or business goes without water.

Our Story

Smart Water was founded in 2024 in response to the growing water access challenges facing urban and peri-urban communities in Kenya. During the Nairobi water rationing crisis, our founder — a resident of Kilimani — spent three days without piped water and struggled to find a reliable, affordable water vendor. That frustration sparked an idea: a digital platform to make water delivery simple, transparent, and fast.

Today, Smart Water serves over 500 customers across Nairobi, Kiambu, Nakuru, Machakos, and Mombasa. We work with more than 20 licensed water vendors to ensure quality, competitive pricing, and reliable delivery — all bookable in minutes from your phone or computer.

Our Objectives

🤝 Connect

Link customers directly with verified, licensed water vendors in their area.

💧 Improve Access

Make clean water accessible to every household and business, regardless of location.

📦 Efficient Distribution

Use technology to streamline water supply chains and reduce delivery time.

💰 Fair Pricing

Provide transparent, competitive pricing to protect customers from price gouging.

🛡️ Quality Assurance

Partner only with vendors who meet our water quality and service standards.

🌱 Sustainability

Promote responsible water use and support community water conservation efforts.
